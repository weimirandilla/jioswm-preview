Article of Faith

Jesus Is Our Shield Worldwide Ministries, founded by the Man of God, Apostle Renato D. Carillo, is an ever-growing ministry with hundreds of established chapters all over the world. It has been a blessing to numerous lives and to the Body of Christ worldwide. Its main church is located at 1069 EDSA, Brgy. Katipunan, Quezon City, Philippines. From the street to the nations of the world, it has reached a significant milestone of 33 years in service to the Lord.


Let us fill our homes, our surroundings with God's resources to help preserve God's presence in our everyday lives. Let us continue to spread the Word of God and be a blessing to the world for Jesus is our shield all over this NATION! and Jesus is our shield all over the WORLD

These we believe that:

1

The bible is the only infallible and authoritative written Word of God, inspired with the Holy Spirit. (2 Timothy 3:16-17)

2

There is one God eternally exist in three persons, the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. (Deut. 6:4, 1 John 5:7, 2 Cor. 13:14, Matt. 3:13-17, 28:19)

3

We believe that man is created in the image and likeness of God, but man fell because of sin. (Rom. 3:10, 5:12)

4

Christ paid the price on the cross for the redemption of man. (John 3:16, Rom. 5:8)

5

Man cannot save himself and that he can only be saved through faith due to the redemptive works of Christ. (Eph. 2:8-9)

6

Through repentance and acceptance to Jesus as Lord and personal Savior, man can freely enjoy the forgiveness of God. (John 1:12, Acts 3:19)

7

Unless a man is born again, he cannot enter the Kingdom of God. His spirit was being regenerated through the Holy Spirit. (John 3:3)

8

Water baptism in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit and the Lordâ€™s Supper through Holy Communion are one of the ordinances, which the Lord gave for the Church. (Matt. 28:19, 1 Cor. 11:23-26)

9

The initial evidence that a Christian has been baptized in the Holy Spirit is speaking in tongues. (Acts 2:4, Acts 19:6, Acts 10:44-46)

10

There are nine fruits and gifts of the Holy Spirit, which every believer of Christ should possess. (1 Cor. 12:1-11, Gal. 5:22)

12

Miracle never ceases and divine healing can be received in answer to the prayer of faith. Christ has the power to heal the sick. (Exo. 15:26, Mark 11:22, Mark 9:19, Luke 18:27)

13

Rapture will take place at any moment, Jesus will return immanently to take His True Church. (1 Thes. 4:16-18, 1 Thes5:1-2, Acts 1:11, John 14:1-4)

14

The method that God ordained to support His work and to spread His Word through tithes and offerings. (Mal. 3:10, 2 Cor. 9:7)

15

Perfection of the Church - living like Jesus - will happen through perfect obedience to God and to His word. (Matt. 5:48, 1 Cor. 11:1, 2 Cor. 13:9 and 11, Eph. 5:25-27, Col. 1:28, 1Thes. 5:23-24, 1 John 2:6, 1 Peter 2:20-21, Heb. 6:2 and 12:2)

16

There are five ministry gift that God gave to the Church, and the ministry of the prophets and apostles still exist today. (Eph. 2:20, 3:5 and 4:7-11, 1 Cor. 4:9 and 12:28)
